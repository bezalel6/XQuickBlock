Privacy Policy for XTerminate Chrome Extension

At XTerminate, accessible from https://github.com/bezalel6/XTerminate, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

XTerminate is designed with privacy in mind. Our extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not store any data on external servers
  • Only processes data locally within your browser

How We Use Your Information

XTerminate operates entirely locally on your device. The extension:

  • Processes web page content locally to identify and block unwanted elements
  • Stores your preferences and settings locally in your browser
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

Data Storage

All data used by XTerminate is stored locally in your browser's storage. This includes:

  • Your extension settings and preferences
  • Custom block rules you create
  • No personal or browsing data is stored

Third-Party Services

XTerminate does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. We do not integrate with any external APIs or services that would collect your data.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can uninstall the extension at any time
  • You can clear all stored data through your browser settings
  • You can modify or delete your custom block rules at any time

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's documentation and GitHub repository.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository at https://github.com/bezalel6/XTerminate.
