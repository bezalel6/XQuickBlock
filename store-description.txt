XTerminator

Take back control of your Twitter feed with XTerminator! This powerful extension adds quick-access mute and block buttons directly to user elements, making it easier than ever to manage your feed.

Key Features:
• One-click mute and block buttons right where you need them
• Control-click to remove multiple accounts at once
• Skip X's endless confirmation dialogs
• Automatically detect and handle promoted tweets
• Hide or block any ad on your feed
• Automatically hide premium content prompts
• Block upgrade interruptions

Privacy First:
• No data collection
• No tracking
• Everything happens in your browser
• No external API calls

Make Twitter semi-usable again with XTerminator - the simple, effective way to clean up your feed!
